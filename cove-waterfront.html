The Setting

The kind of view no florist could improve on.

The Cove faces open water on three sides. There is no road behind it, no neighbour in frame — only the lawn, the bluff, and the long silver line where the bay meets the sky.

Tides are charted a year ahead, so your ceremony lands at the exact hour the light turns gold and the water goes still. Some things, even here, are left to nothing but timing.
